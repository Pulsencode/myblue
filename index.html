About us

Who we are

My Blue is a gift and personalization studio focused on turning everyday moments into keepsakes. From photo frames and polaroid rings to fully customized hampers, letters, and décor — every piece is assembled with care for birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, and surprises that matter.

Whether you need a budget-friendly hamper or a signature My Blue special set, we work with you on theme, colours, and contents so the gift feels personal — not generic.

Our mission

Why we do this

  • Thoughtful over trendy We choose quality presentation and meaningful details, not mass-produced filler.
  • Built around you Custom hampers, letters, magazines, and bouquets are priced and planned around your brief.
  • Clear, honest pricing List prices are on our shop section; shipping and extras are confirmed before you pay.
  • Easy to order Call or WhatsApp with your picks — we guide you from selection to delivery.
